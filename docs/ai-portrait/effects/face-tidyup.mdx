## Application Scenarios

- **Face Beauty Camera**: The photos taken are processed with effects such as big eyes, thin face, thin chin, mouth shape and thin nose to enhance the social image of the person.
- **Live short video**: The anchors in the live broadcast room use face beauty technology to make themselves more attractive and have more fans' attention.
- **Short video production**: Users make their own short videos and use face beauty technology to enhance the viewing effect.
- **Camera and other APP**: Used in cameras and other video-type APPs to enhance visual beauty and increase fun.

## Featured Advantages

- **Natural effect**: Based on deep learning algorithm, a variety of beauty effects are natural and not abrupt.
- **Support multiple beauty shapes**: Support various effects such as big eyes, thin face, thin chin, mouth shape, thin nose, etc.
- **Custom adjustment**: Support custom adjustment parameters to help enhance personal image.

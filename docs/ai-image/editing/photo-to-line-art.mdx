## Application Scenarios

- **Classroom and family activities**: Turn photos into coloring pages for easy printing and hands-on creativity.
- **Crafts and templates**: Produce clean outlines for tracing, cutting, engraving, or tattoo sketch references.
- **People, pets, and illustration**: Preserve key features while simplifying background noise for clearer coloring.
- **Events and content production**: Batch-generate consistent coloring assets for campaigns and social content.

## Featured Advantages

- **Line-art friendly**: Extracts clean contours and reduces noise for coloring and printing.
- **Format support**: Accepts JPEG and PNG inputs.
